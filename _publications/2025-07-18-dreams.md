**DREAMS** is a hierarchical, multi-agent framework for density functional theory (DFT) simulation. It combines a central large-language-model (LLM) planner agent with domain-specific agents for atomistic structure generation, systematic DFT convergence testing, high-performance-computing (HPC) scheduling, and error handling — automating first-principles materials simulation from natural-language intent to validated results.
