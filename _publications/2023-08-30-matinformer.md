We introduce the **Materials Informatics Transformer (MatInFormer)**, a language model for materials property prediction. The model learns the grammar of crystallography through the tokenization of pertinent space-group information, and adapts to task-specific data such as Metal–Organic Frameworks (MOFs). Through attention visualization, we uncover the key features the model prioritizes during prediction. MatInFormer is validated across 14 distinct datasets, demonstrating its potential for high-throughput screening through accurate materials property prediction.
